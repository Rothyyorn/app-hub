# Minimalism in UI Design

Minimalism isn't just about white space; it's about clarity and focus. By removing unnecessary elements, we help users achieve their goals more efficiently.

## Principles of Minimalist Design

1. **Clarity**: Every element should have a purpose.
2. **Hierarchy**: Use size, weight, and color to guide the eye.
3. **Consistency**: A predictable interface is a usable interface.

> "Design is not just what it looks like and feels like. Design is how it works." - Steve Jobs

Focus on the essentials.
